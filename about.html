Profile

Rocamora Works is the portfolio of Joshua Rocamora, bringing together architecture, photography, and research through a shared interest in space, atmosphere, and the details that shape everyday experience.

The site is organized as a growing archive: selected design work, photographic series, and research notes can expand over time while keeping the navigation simple and direct.
